# Bitcoin-Market-Sentiment-Analysis

## dataset 

# fear greed dataset 
4 specific columns 
map market emotions over time 

timestamp unix - exact seconds as it was started 

value : A numerical score ranging from 0 to 100.

0–24 = Extreme Fear

25–49 = Fear

50–54 = Neutral

55–74 = Greed

75–100 = Extreme Greed

date is human readable !! 

1) dataset proves markets are driven by intense emotion as it divide days into periods of panic
2) best buying windows can be identified well when index bottoms out in single digits when everyone panic market is usually at floor
3) prolonged streak of greed ( values above 60 to 70) serving as warning sign to cash out
4) how fast market mood flips sudden jumps from fear to greed over couple of dats and aggressive capital inflows fueled by Fear of missing out
5) Buy the deep fear, sell the extreme greed, and use the index momentum to time market reversals.
